For each question, you will see 6 options of how to distribute jobs across periods. The leftmost response button indicates that all 100,000 jobs are created {{curr_block.text_total_end}}. The rightmost response button indicates that all jobs are created {{curr_block.text_delay_start}}. The buttons in between represent different combinations of some jobs created {{curr_block.text_total_end}} and some jobs created {{curr_block.text_delay_start}}. So, you decide how to distribute jobs created between the two periods. In some questions, the maximum number of jobs created {{curr_block.text_delay_start}} is larger than the maximum number {{curr_block.text_total_end}}; in others, it is smaller or the same. So the total number of jobs created over the two period varies across questions.
