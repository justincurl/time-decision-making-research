PAST: {{later_time}} and {{earlier_time}}


For each question, you will see 10 green blocks, each representing 50,000 jobs created {{later_time}}. After your first click on any of the blocks, a blue horizontal bar will appear. By dragging the blue bar up and down, you can move each green block between {{later_time}} and {{earlier_time}}. The blue bar lists the number of jobs created in each period. We would like you to arrange the blocks so they show the distribution of jobs across the two periods that you think represents the best economy.

In some questions, moving a block of 50,000 from {{later_time}} to {{earlier_time}} leads to more than 50,000 jobs created earlier. In other questions, it leads to fewer than 50,000 jobs created earlier or leaves the number unchanged. So, the total number of jobs that could have been created over the two periods varies across questions.
